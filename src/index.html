project: Strand Series
year: 2016
material: Wood strands with BioResin, LED strip lighting
collaborators: Canadian Speciality Castings
location: Toronto, ON

A ceiling suspended light fixture that utilizes the transparency and malleability of thin wood stands. The strand light features a unique infusion process that replaces harmful formaldehyde glues with eco friendly bio resins. LEDs provide natural white light, illuminating the wood composite to reveal a beautiful grain texture adding an ambience to any space.
